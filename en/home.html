This is your assistance tool for artillery in the Foxhole game world. Here you can calculate cannon shots, adjust the firing angle and get additional information about the artillery.

Foxhole is a multiplayer online strategy game set in a world of persistent warfare. A artillery plays a crucial role in this game, allowing players to attack distant targets and play a strategic role in conquering territories. Artillery includes various types of weapons, such as cannons, mortars and rockets, which can be used to attack enemy bases, block roads and support allied advances. support allied advances. Players need to coordinate their efforts and communication in order to effectively use the artillery and unbalance the battlefield in their favour. The precision and strategic positioning of artillery weapons are fundamental to successful military operations in Foxhole.
